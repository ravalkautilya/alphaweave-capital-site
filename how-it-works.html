Why it matters

A signal is only the start of the decision.

AlphaWeave Capital separates the spark, the decision, the trade, and the realized result. That makes performance easier to explain because the team can see whether the idea was wrong, the decision was late, or the execution got in the way.

AlphaWeave Capital is research and decision-support software. It is not investment advice.
