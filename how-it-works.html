Why It Matters

Signals are not the same as governed decisions.

AlphaWeave Capital separates signal generation, role judgment, risk review, execution, and realized outcome feedback. That separation makes it possible to diagnose whether performance came from the strategy, asset selection, execution timing, or sizing.

AlphaWeave Capital is research and decision-support software. It is not investment advice.
