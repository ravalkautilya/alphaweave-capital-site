Basket Sleeves

Market-neutral reasoning without losing the legs.

AlphaWeave Capital handles active long and short assets as first-class context. A sleeve is a portfolio mandate or allocation bucket; a market-neutral sleeve evaluates spread behavior, hedge integrity, and realized contribution.

Basket-Aware Logic

Built for relative-value decisions.

Example

Long winners, short hedges, reviewed together.

A market-neutral sleeve can evaluate an active long leg and short leg together instead of treating each asset as a standalone directional bet. The result is a cleaner view of whether the basket helped the sleeve.

AlphaWeave Capital is research and decision-support software. It is not investment advice.
