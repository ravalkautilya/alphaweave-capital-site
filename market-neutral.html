Treat relative-value trades like relationships, not tickers.

Example

Long thesis, short hedge, one combined story.

A market-neutral sleeve is a portfolio mandate for a balanced basket. Instead of judging each asset alone, AlphaWeave Capital asks whether the relationship worked: did the long side help, did the short side hedge, did beta stay within mandate, and did the spread improve the portfolio?

Adaptive neutrality

Do not assume the pair still works. Re-validate it.

Traditional market-neutral workflows can treat a pair as valid because it was valid before. AlphaWeave Capital keeps re-checking whether the relationship is stable, degrading, or improving, then adjusts basket construction, strategy choice, and conviction sizing.
