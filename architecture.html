Architecture

Transparent AI for teams that need control.

AlphaWeave Capital breaks the allocation process into visible reviewers, deterministic controls, and audit-ready reports so AI can help without becoming a black box. The system mirrors a trading desk: evidence, review, risk, portfolio construction, execution, and memory each have a defined role.

VisibleEach reviewer has a clear job and handoff.
ControlledRules handle sleeve constraints, risk, parsing, caching, and routing.
AuditablePrompts, responses, cache matches, and outcomes are inspectable.

Agent Chain

Specialized reviewers, not one giant black box.

An agent is a software reviewer responsible for one part of the workflow. An LLM, or large language model, is an AI model trained to read and generate text; AlphaWeave Capital uses LLMs where judgment and summarization help, while rules handle the parts that must be exact.

Single-bar decision flow

Every evaluation follows the same route from data to memory.

At each bar, AlphaWeave Capital loads data, selects assets, selects strategies, generates raw signals, triages whether deeper AI review is needed, converts conviction into portfolio intent, applies risk controls, and stores the outcome for future learning.
